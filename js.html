Common Traditions

1. Lunar New Year

During Lunar new year, it is a common tradition among family member adults to share red pockets to the children of each other. This pocket consists of money and the meaning of this is to bring good luck to them.

2. Moon Festval

During Moon Festval, families gather outside at night to watch the full moon, as it is a beautiful sight to behold. A unique snack create for this event is called the mooncake. it is sweet with diffrent kinds of fillings inside of it, but the most noticable feature is the patterns on the top of it, often with a symbol that represents good luck and fortune.

3. Dragon Dance

During the Dragon Dance, famalies gather outside to watch the eventful dance The dances is also acompinied by lots of music playing as well as firecrackers and other party makers.
